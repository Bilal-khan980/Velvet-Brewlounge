About Us

Established in 2024, Velvet Brewlounge combines the art of coffee brewing with culinary excellence. Our space is designed to be your perfect escape, whether for a morning coffee or an evening wine.

We source our beans ethically from small-scale farmers around the world, ensuring each cup not only tastes exceptional but also supports sustainable farming practices.
